How We Work

A process built for clarity.

Five stages. Clear deliverables at each step. No black boxes, no surprise invoices, no vague updates. You always know what is happening, why, and what comes next.

The Pipeline

Five stages, zero surprises.

Every project at CODEV2E Studio moves through the same five stages. Each stage has a clear deliverable, a clear sign-off, and a clear next step — so you are never left wondering where things stand.

Stages can be compressed or expanded based on scope, but they always happen in this order. Skipping stages is how good animation goes bad.

Stage 01 · 1–3 days
Discover
We dig into your story, audience, references, and goals. No assumptions, no templates. The output is a creative brief that we both sign off on — the source of truth for everything that follows.
Kickoff call Reference gathering Creative brief
Stage 02 · 3–7 days
Develop
Style frames, storyboards, and an animatic that locks the direction before animation begins. This is where we catch problems cheaply — not after 200 frames are already drawn.
Style frames Storyboard Animatic Sign-off
Stage 03 · 1–8 weeks
Animate
The craft happens here. Performance, timing, motion, and effects — brought to life frame by frame. You see progress at regular intervals, never just at the end.
Blocking Rough animation Polish pass Weekly reviews
Stage 04 · 2–5 days
Finish
Compositing, color grading, sound design sync, and final render. This is where everything comes together — the polish that separates good animation from great animation.
Compositing Color grade Sound sync Final render
Stage 05 · 1 day
Deliver
Final files delivered in every format you need — including source files, project files, and a style guide so your team can extend the work without us if needed.
Master files Source files Style guide Handoff call

FAQ

Questions we hear a lot.

Quick answers to the most common questions. If yours is not here, just ask us directly — we reply fast and we do not bite.

Ask your question →
How fast can you start? +

Usually within 1–2 weeks of signing off on the brief. For rush projects we will try to make room, but we will never over-promise on timeline.

What is your minimum project size? +

We do not have a hard minimum, but most projects start around the cost of a focused single deliverable — a logo loop, a teaser, a turnaround. We will tell you honestly if your scope is too small for us to do well.

Do you sign NDAs? +

Yes. We sign mutual NDAs before any sensitive material is shared. Your IP stays yours — we treat every project like it is our own original work.

Can you work with our existing art or rigs? +

Usually yes, depending on the format and quality. Send us what you have and we will assess it honestly — including whether it would be faster to start fresh.

Do you do revenue-share or original IP? +

For the right original project, we are open to hybrid deals — reduced fee in exchange for a stake. Pitch us your idea and we will talk.

Ready to start Stage 01?

Discovery is free. Tell us what you are building and we will tell you if we are the right studio for it — no commitment, no pressure.
