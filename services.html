Capabilities

Everything you need to animate a story.

Six core disciplines that cover the full pipeline — from first sketch to final render. Pick one, or let us handle the entire journey.

01 / SERVICE

2D Animation

Hand-crafted 2D animation for shorts, loops, explainers, music videos, and original series. We work both frame-by-frame (traditional) and rigged (cut-out) — choosing the right technique for the look you want.

Frame-by-frame Rigged cut-out Loop animation Character performance Effects animation Lip sync
02 / SERVICE

3D Animation

Stylised 3D animation, modeling, rigging, and lighting built for character work, product visuals, and immersive worlds. We lean toward a hand-crafted look — never the generic "3D render" feel.

Character animation Modeling & rigging Stylised look dev Lighting Product viz Simulation
03 / SERVICE

Motion Graphics

Title sequences, brand bumpers, kinetic typography, UI motion, and explainer videos — engineered to deliver your message in seconds without losing soul.

Title sequences Brand bumpers Kinetic type Explainers UI / UX motion Logo animation
04 / SERVICE

Character Design

Original characters with personality. We deliver turnaround sheets, expression packs, pose libraries, and ready-to-rig art — built to survive the entire production, not just the pitch deck.

Concept design Turnarounds Expression sheets Pose libraries Rig-ready art Style guides
05 / SERVICE

Storyboarding

Cinematic storyboards and animatics that map pacing, blocking, and emotion before a single frame is animated. Catch problems early, save budget, and align everyone on the same vision.

Storyboard panels Animatics Shot lists Camera blocking Pacing notes Pitch decks
06 / SERVICE

Visual Development

World-building, color scripts, environment design, and style frames that define the look of your story before animation begins. The visual blueprint every project needs.

World-building Color scripts Environment design Style frames Mood boards Prop design
