Inside the Studio

A studio built for storytellers.

CODEV2E Studio is an independent animation house — small, focused, and obsessed with craft. We exist to give creators, brands, and dreamers a place where their stories can be animated with intention.

Our Story

Born from necessity.

CODEV2E Studio was founded on a simple belief: animation should feel alive. Not polished into oblivion. Not templated. Alive — with weight, rhythm, and intention behind every single frame.

We are a newly launched studio, and we own that. No bloated roster, no inherited pipeline baggage, no aging brand to maintain. What we bring instead is full attention — every project gets the same care we would give our own original work.

The name CODEV2E comes from "code" + "v2" + "e" — a nod to iteration, to versioning, to the idea that good animation is never done on the first pass. It is refined, again and again, until it moves the way it should.
